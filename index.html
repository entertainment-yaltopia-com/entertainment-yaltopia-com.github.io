About Us

“Happiness can be found even in the darkest of times, if one only remembers to turn on the light.” Dumbledore

A friendship that spans decades, and still keeps on going. A bunch of guys that like computers, coding, designing, gaming, and movies. Bunch of guys who lose their temper here and there manifest in the abuse of keyboards and mouses mostly due to broken dreams (Journey to Plat). As a group we always appreciated the fact that we were able to find each other to discuss about things we like in detail. That was the huge drive behind the creation of this platform, connect with us and other likeminded people here.

May the Force be with You!

#KappaAngles , KuntaSensie , Kolo Podcast , #Melata , Vaseline Man .. and many more. Moments like these are what we are about even though some came at the expense of our good old friends. It is all done for all of you to enjoy the competitive experience, and sharing our personalities with you guys. Even though the tournament happened during a terrible time, our community has risen, we hope something positive has come out of it. We hope to continue on with this journey and bring you content that can bring joy to your day. Reinforced with a bright day you will tackle whatever is in front of you knowing that every week we got your back when it is time to relax.
